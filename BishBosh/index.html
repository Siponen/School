Bish-Bosh 2.0

This is a small script created as a school assignment.

BishBosh outputs a range of numbers, those numbers are divided with two specific integers.
If the BishNumber is divisible without any remainder then the output will be 'Bish'.
If the BoshNumber is divisible without any remainder then the output will be 'Bosh'.
If both numbers are divisible without any remainder then the output will be 'Bish-Bosh'.

BishNumber, BoshNumber and range must be larger than 0.
